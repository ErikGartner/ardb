Ardb is a tool for V:tes Players

Vampire: The Eternal Struggle(formerly Jyhad) is an exciting trading card game of Gothic intrigue based on White Wolf's storytelling game Vampire: The Masquerade.

Enter a world of majestry and mystery as you assume the role of an ancient vampire. As your minions descend into a passionate maelstrom of relentless politics and nefarious conspiracies you must wage war against your opponents. Draining their precious resources while mastering the societies of the living and the undead. The game is designed for two or more players; each player needs a deck to play. The complete set contains over four hundred cards and features full color illustrations by some of the genre's most prominent artists. Join the Eternal Struggle, where blood is your weakness... and your ultimate weapon!
